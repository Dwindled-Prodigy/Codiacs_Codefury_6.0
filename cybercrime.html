Introduction

Welcome to the Contact Cyber Police portal. If you have encountered a cybercrime or suspicious online activity, we are here to help. Please use the form below to report the incident to us.

Procedures

  1. Fill out the form with accurate details regarding the cyber incident.
  2. If you have any evidence, please attach it using the provided file input.
  3. Submit the form for our investigation.
  4. Our cybercrime unit will review your report and take appropriate action.
  5. You will receive a confirmation message once your report is submitted.
