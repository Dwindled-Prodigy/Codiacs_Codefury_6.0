The Wise Old Farmer

Once upon a time in a small village, there lived a wise old farmer. One year, a terrible drought hit the village, and the crops withered away. The other farmers panicked and gave up, but the wise old farmer kept planting his seeds, even though the land was dry. When asked why he continued to plant, he replied, "I believe in the rain. I believe it will come eventually, and my seeds will grow." His unwavering faith inspired others to keep trying too. Sure enough, the rain eventually came, and the wise old farmer's crops flourished. The moral of the story: Have faith in your efforts, even when circumstances seem tough.
